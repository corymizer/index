A collection of signs created during my time at Signature Sign Company and Signs PDQ, both in Cleveland, Ohio.

Flats East Bank

This signage sits atop the Flats East Bank apartment building on the East Bank of the Cuyahoga River in downtown Cleveland, Ohio. We designed the letters using the iconic Domino Sugar sign in Baltimore as inspiration. The 'FLATS' stands 10 foot tall and the 'EAST BANK' stands 6 foot tall. There was existing framework, but we at Signature Sign designed a 'faux' framework to give it an older feel. The sign is lit with double strand LED to mimic old school neon.

Alley Cat

Alley Cat Oyster Bar is a new restaurant owned by Zack Bruell in the Flats East Bank. The signage we created was a custom font, fabricated aluminum channel letters with accompanying fish logo. We did a large scale logo over the 'terrace' and a smaller one with the fish logo on the front of the building, as well as a fish logo by the door. All signs were aluminum channel letters.

Crop

This is the signage for Crop Sticks, Crop Rocks, and Crop On Air, in the Flats East Bank in Cleveland, Ohio. Breaking away from the formal feel of the Ohio City restaurant, these new restaurants have more of a fun family feel and each on has it's own theme/menu. The 'Crop Rocks" letters are fabricated aluminum channel letters, while the 'On Air' and 'Crop Sicks' signs are custom built LED illuminated cabinets.
