Cleveland Soccer Club is a new club in the National Premier Soccer League, the 4th division of American soccer. After working for 3 years for AFC Cleveland, who ended up folding, I became Creative Director for Cleveland SC. My duties with the club include creating brand guidelines, creating and executing gameday graphics and advertising material, and managing the creative team within the club.

This site is in production for use in next year's NPSL Season. The site is mostly designed using HTML5 and CSS3, but includes elements of Javascript and Jquery in the matchday countdown and the dropdown menus.
